What is ha'W'ckathon ?

HA’W’KATHON is a district-wide project by GEC Wayanad to give students a platform to address some of the critical issues we encounter on a daily basis and foster a culture of product innovation and a problem-solving mindset.

What is the Focus Area ?

  • TRANSPORTATION
  • AGRICULTURE
  • HEALTHCARE
  • FOREST
  • TOURISM
  • DISASTER MANAGEMENT
  • RURAL DEVELOPMENT
  • ROBOTICS
  • CYBER SECURITY

Who can Participate ?

Any Undergraduate, Higher Secondary or High school student who is currently studing in Wayanad.
